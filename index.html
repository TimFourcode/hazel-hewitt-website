Hidden messages resurface, refusing to remain buried.

Ebley, an abandoned and derelict station,

has kept its secrets for years and it's not

prepared to give them up easily. Nor would

it seem are the local village residents,

least of all Rose.

Paranormal investigators: Sarah and Simon

know that time is against them.

Will one week be enough to uncover the

truth about this quaint village that is

intertwined with intrigue and mystery?

Only time will tell.

About the Author

Louise H Archer (pseudonym) writes Paranormal fiction (Ghost stories). Her debut novel, ROSE, has gained a lot of interest in the UK and internationally. At Hugendubel, in Hannover, Germany, it has earned her a manager's in-store recommendation.

Keep an eye out for future publications.
