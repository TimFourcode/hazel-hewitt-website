About the Author

Born in Hampshire, England, Louise H. Archer writes paranormal fiction under a carefully chosen pseudonym. Her stories blend ghost tales with warmth and unexpected humour — a combination that has earned her a staff recommendation at Hugendubel Hannover, where ROSE sits alongside international bestsellers.

Her debut novel ROSE is set in and around the atmospheric Ebley Station — a Victorian railway building that becomes the backdrop for secrets, mystery, and things that refuse to stay buried. The book is published by Olympia Publishers and available across Europe.

A second novel has been confirmed with a new publisher. More details coming soon.
